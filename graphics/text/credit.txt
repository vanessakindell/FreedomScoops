Freedoom Scoops is a collaborative
effort to create a complete
free content game. It is the combined work
of many, many different talented
game content makers around the world
more than is possible to list
their roles on this page alone.
Thanks go to everyone who has contributed
and helped to make this project a reality.

Freedom Flakes is distributed as
free software under the terms
of the BSD license, and is
under continual development and
improvement. If you like the project and
want to help make it better, visit the
Freedom Flakes website:

        https://FreedomFlakes.github.io/

Thanks for playing!
